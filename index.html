Our Services

Comprehensive, compassionate physiotherapy & wellness services.

At Align Asya Physiotherapy Centre, Mira Road, Mumbai, we provide comprehensive, evidence-based physiotherapy care using state-of-the-art facilities and modern treatment modalities. Our expert physiotherapists focus on relieving pain, restoring movement, and enhancing overall quality of life through personalized treatment plans.

We offer a wide range of services including manual therapy, therapeutic exercises, electrotherapy, and hydrotherapy, ensuring faster recovery and long-term results for patients of all age groups.

Musculoskeletal Physiotherapy

We specialize in the diagnosis and treatment of muscle, joint, and spine-related conditions. Our musculoskeletal physiotherapy programs help reduce pain, improve mobility, and prevent future injuries.

Conditions treated include:
  • • Back and neck pain
  • • Shoulder, knee, and joint disorders
  • • Sports injuries
  • • Arthritis and posture-related issues
  • • Post-fracture and post-surgical rehabilitation

Neurological Rehabilitation

Our neurological rehabilitation services are designed to help patients regain independence and functional ability following neurological conditions or injuries. We use task-specific training and advanced therapeutic techniques to promote neuro-recovery.

We treat conditions such as:
  • • Stroke (paralysis)
  • • Parkinson's disease
  • • Multiple sclerosis
  • • Spinal cord injuries
  • • Nerve injuries and neuropathies

Women's Health & Antenatal Care

We provide specialized physiotherapy care tailored to women's unique health needs across different life stages. Our antenatal and postnatal programs focus on comfort, strength, and recovery.

Our services include:
  • • Antenatal exercise programs
  • • Pregnancy-related back and pelvic pain management
  • • Postnatal rehabilitation
  • • Pelvic floor strengthening
  • • Post-delivery recovery and posture correction

Geriatric Physiotherapy

Our geriatric physiotherapy services aim to promote active aging, independence, and improved balance in older adults. We focus on safe, gentle, and effective therapies to enhance mobility and confidence.

Key areas include:
  • • Balance and fall-prevention training
  • • Joint stiffness and pain management
  • • Osteoporosis and arthritis care
  • • Strength and mobility enhancement
  • • Post-surgical rehabilitation

Pediatric Physiotherapy

We offer compassionate and specialized physiotherapy care for infants and children to support healthy growth and development. Our child-friendly approach ensures comfort and effective outcomes.

Conditions treated include:
  • • Developmental delays
  • • Cerebral palsy
  • • Postural and gait abnormalities
  • • Congenital and neurological conditions
  • • Sports and activity-related injuries

Wellness & Preventive Care

Our Wellness & Preventive Care programs are designed to help individuals maintain optimal physical health, prevent injuries, and enhance overall well-being through proactive physiotherapy.

Our wellness services include:
  • • Posture assessment and correction
  • • Ergonomic advice for workplace and daily activities
  • • Movement screening and injury prevention programs
  • • Flexibility, strength, and core stability training
  • • Lifestyle and fitness guidance for long-term health

About Dr. Renu Saroj

Dr. Renu Saroj is a dedicated physiotherapist with extensive experience in musculoskeletal and sports rehabilitation. Her approach is patient-centred, evidence-based and focused on returning you safely to the activities you love. Our expert physiotherapist Dr. Renu Saroj and her team in Mumbai is a skilled healthcare professional trained in various therapeutic techniques to restore movement, alleviate pain, and enhance overall mobility. Physiotherapists treat a number of musculoskeletal conditions - a broad term that encompasses damage to bones, muscles, ligaments, tendons and nerves. Because these conditions are so wide ranging, they affect from children (as in juvenile rheumatoid arthritis) to the elderly (as in osteoarthritis) and everyone else who may suffer an injury. Other conditions which fall under this heading include carpal tunnel syndrome, muscular dystrophy, fibromyalgia and osteomyelitis (bone infection), to name a few. Many patients recover their strength and ability to function again through physiotherapy treatment.
